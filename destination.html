Explore the beauty of Italy

Home to some of the greatest artworks, historical monuments and food on the planet, and with the kind of varied landscapes that you often have to cross continents to see, Italy elates, inspires and moves its visitors like few other countries. From its art- and architecture-stuffed cities to its astonishing diversity of natural landscapes – dramatic coastlines, serene lakes, pristine mountains, and those famous rolling hills – picking just a handful of destinations to visit in this wonderful country is no easy task. So how do you decide where to go? It all depends on what you’re looking for in your Italian odyssey, whether that’s history, art, beaches, hiking or even food. No matter your passion, our list of 15 must-see places in Italy should be your starting point.

Explore Helsinki: A Nordic Adventure

Embark on a journey to Helsinki, the vibrant capital of Finland, nestled on the shores of the Baltic Sea. This tour offers an immersive experience into the rich culture, fascinating history, and stunning architecture of this Nordic gem. Helsinki Cathedral: Marvel at the neoclassical grandeur of this iconic landmark dominating the city skyline. Suomenlinna Sea Fortress: Step back in time as you wander through this UNESCO World Heritage site, exploring its historic walls and fortifications. Market Square: Immerse yourself in the bustling atmosphere of Helsinki's vibrant market square, lined with stalls offering local delicacies, handicrafts, and souvenirs. Design District: Discover Helsinki's cutting-edge design scene as you stroll through the trendy streets of the Design District, home to boutiques, galleries, and innovative design studios. Temppeliaukio Church (Rock Church): Be captivated by the unique architecture of this church carved into solid rock, creating a serene and awe-inspiring space.

Discover Paris: The City of Lights

Paris, the enchanting "City of Lights," beckons with its timeless allure and romantic ambiance. From the iconic elegance of the Eiffel Tower to the majestic grandeur of Notre-Dame Cathedral, every corner of this cultural capital exudes charm and sophistication. Delve into the world-renowned art collections at the Louvre Museum, stroll along the picturesque Seine River, and savor exquisite French cuisine at charming sidewalk cafes. Lose yourself in the cobblestone streets of Montmartre, where the spirit of Picasso and Van Gogh lingers, or immerse yourself in the high fashion of the Golden Triangle. Whether admiring historic landmarks, indulging in gourmet delights, or simply basking in the city's romantic atmosphere, Paris promises an unforgettable journey filled with beauty, culture, and timeless elegance.
